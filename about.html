About Us

At Dog Hiring Service, we believe that every dog deserves a loving and active companion. Our mission is to connect dog owners with qualified dog lovers who are ready to provide care, exercise, and companionship.

Founded in 2023, our team is passionate about dogs and dedicated to ensuring the best experiences for both dogs and their caregivers. We carefully vet all our dog handlers to ensure safety and reliability.

Our Team

Our team consists of dog lovers and trained professionals who understand the unique needs of different breeds. We are here to help you find the perfect match for your furry friend.
